Instructions

Welcome to Beetleships!

A competitive insect discovery game

THE STORY:

  • You are a daring explorer deep in the jungles of Indonesia, searching for undiscovered insect species.
  • You’ve identified five brand-new species — but you’re not alone.
  • A rival explorer is also in the area and has discovered the same rare insects.
  • Only one of you can claim the scientific glory.

YOUR MISSION:

  • Find and capture all five of your rival’s insects before they find yours.
  • Be the first player to locate and capture all five insect species hidden on your opponent’s jungle grid and you win the game!

HOW TO PLAY:

  1. Start the game: Each player is given a jungle grid with five hidden insect species, randomly placed.
  2. Take turns exploring: Player 1 goes first, then players alternate turns.
  3. Search the jungle: On your turn, click a square on your opponent’s grid to search for insects.
  4. Hit or Miss: If you get a hit, you're on to something! If you get a miss, you’ve searched empty jungle.
  5. End your turn: After your search, both boards are covered and a button appears for the next player to press.
  6. Switch players: The next player should only click the button once the other player has looked away.
  7. Capture species: When all squares of an insect are revealed, that species is captured.
  8. Win the game: The first explorer to capture all five of their rival’s insect species wins!
